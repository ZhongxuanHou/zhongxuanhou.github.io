Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

The vertical wind turbine ss a type of wind turbine where the main rotor is set transverse to the wind while the main components are located at the base of the turbine. The comparison between the horizontal and vertical axis wind turbin is given in the figure below. Two types of VAWT are also introduced.

Savonius

A Savonius vertical-axis wind turbine is a slow rotating, high torque machine that is ideal for driving pumps.

Darrieus

A Darrieus is a high speed, low torque machine suitable for generating alternating current (AC) electricity
