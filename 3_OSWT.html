Offshore Wind Turbine

Offshore wind turbine is constucted in the ocean to harvest wind energy. Due to the cost of construction for the grid and the foundation, an offshore wind farm is what normally seen.

Advantages

There are multiple reason for choosing offshore wind turbine than onshore wind turbine. Two main reasons are the steady and strong wind, and electricity needs.

Strong and Steady Wind

Comparing to onshore, offshore location can harvest higher wind speed. And the wind is more stable due to no obstables.

Electricity Needs

A lot of the population dense areas are located in coastal areas, which mean higher electricity needs. The construction of offshore wind farm wont take any land from the cities, while producing reliable energy.

Disadvantages

When considering the disadvantages, there are the construction difficulty, extreme wind condition.

Construction Difficulty

To build a robust wind farm offshore requires careful investication of the seabed condition and the costal environment. The foundation and the tower also need to resist erosion from the seawater.

Extreme Wind Condition

Costal areas often experience strong wind gust condition. And the potential threats from hurricanes and storms can be dangerous to the wind turbine structure.
