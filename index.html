Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine

The most common type of wind turbine that constructed onshore. The height of the horizontal wind turbine ranges from 40m to 150m.

Off Shore Wind Turbine

Offshore wind turbine is also preferred. Higer wind speed can be achieved offshore comparing to onshore due to less obstacles and terrain changes.

Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

The vertical wind turbine is typically very small comparing to the horizontal wind turbine. Although it has lower efficiency, its construction cost is much cheaper and can be even installed on the rooftop.
